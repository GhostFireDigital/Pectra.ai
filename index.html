What is Pectra.ai?

Pectra.ai is a community-led initiative exploring how artificial intelligence and user-first design can shape Ethereum’s future during the Pectra upgrade cycle. We focus on account abstraction, onboarding simplification, and AI-enhanced developer/user tools for DApps.

In Development

AI Wallet Walkthrough Generator – Guide new users through contract wallets & L2s
Onboarding Simulation Engine – Visual onboarding UX testing for dev teams
UX Audit Toolkit – Heuristics + AI review framework for Ethereum dApps
Community UX Feedback Portal – Submit ideas, vote on design experiments
